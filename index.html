About Me

Mr. Sylvester is a retired professional who has spent the majority of his life working in Navy, first on active duty and then in Civil Service. He has had a long and successful career and is now enjoying his well-deserved retirement years in the beautiful city of Honolulu. Mr. Sylvester is a highly respected member of the community and is known for his kind and generous nature. He is a beloved member of the Arcadia Retirement Residence community and is always willing to lend a helping hand to his fellow residents. He enjoys spending time reading books, watching movies and shows, as well as keeping up with current events. One of Mr. Sylvester’s greatest passions was traveling. He has been fortunate enough to visit many different parts of the world throughout his life and has many wonderful stories to share about his adventures. He believes that travel is one of the best ways to learn about different cultures and broaden one’s perspective. Mr. Sylvester is a devoted family man and enjoys spending time with his children and grandchildren whenever possible. He also values the friendships he has made at Arcadia Retirement Residence and cherishes the ti me he spends with his fellow residents. Overall, a vibrant and engaging member of the Arcadia Retirement Residence community. He continues to live life to the fullest, and his positive outlook on life serves as an inspiration to all those around him.
